What is this?!?

In the spirit of Halloween me and my husband will be watching a horror movie each night. We selected classics from the Nightmare on Elm Street and Friday the 13th franchises and even some recent ones. We mixed them up and prepared a nice calendar for the month of October.

Since I am passionate about data, I will be doing some data analysis on these movies. As since will happen gradually I will be updating the site whenever I come up with something new.
